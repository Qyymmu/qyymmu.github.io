About VOLNET

This is a game in RPG genre that tells us about boy, who's name is Volter

  • Hello, my name is Volter. I'm 14 and I live with mom and grandma. I enjoy reading books and listening to music, also I play ukulele. But I feel lonely. I haven't got any friends. My dad died in an accident when I was only 10 and grandpa died when I was 10 too, cause of problems with heart. My mum is working all day long on work, that she hates. Granny is housewife. She likes to cook. I study at home cuz people at school have beaten me. It's my first note in this notebook, but I'm sure, I'll write in it every day.
